steve jobs

visionary • innovator • legend

1955 – 2011

the man who thought different

steve jobs was an american entrepreneur, inventor, and designer who co-founded apple inc. he revolutionized personal computing, music, mobile phones, tablets, animation, and digital publishing. known for his relentless pursuit of perfection and intuitive design, jobs left an indelible mark on technology and culture.
